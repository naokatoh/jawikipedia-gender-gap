Japanese Wikipedia's Text Analysis

Category: Scientist

The number of articles between female and male scientists is apparent but the content of the article does not show the tangible difference.

Category: Japanese Idols

More than 90% of the articles on Japanese idols are on female idols. The word "graduation" appears frequently in articles about female idols, which means quitting an idol group. The word "graduation" does not appear in male idols, perhaps because they often move on to acting or other entertainment careers as they get older.

Category: Prince and Princess

The princesses' articles frequently use words describing their appearance and youth, such as "beautiful", "very young", and "young", whereas the princes' articles do not.

Category: figure skater

Articles on figure skaters are more numerous for female athletes. Perhaps because figure skating is not necessarily a sport in which masculinity is valued, feminine words such as "beautiful" are used more frequently in articles about men.
